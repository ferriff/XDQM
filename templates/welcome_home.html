Welcome to the Cross/Cupid Data Quality Monitoring

Plots are under the Runs tab, ordered in folders that follows the structure used by the DAQ. Data of a run are split in chunks numbered in the order of acquisition time statring from 000.

Tips

  • To browse the runs, click on "Runs"
  • To display the latest acquired data (latest chunk of latest run), click on "Latest".
  • To jump to a specific run chunk, you can enter its identifier the format RUN#.CHUNK# in the Run.chunk field. If CHUNK# is omitted, the last chunk of the run is used.
  • To compare the plots of two runs, check the box in front of "compare to" and enter the reference RUN#.CHUNK# in the field that follows.
